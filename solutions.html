Primary Project Case Study

Project SteelVision: Spatial Intelligence at the Edge.

The Problem Space: Modern asset verification requires high-reliability spatial awareness, but cloud dependency introduces latency and privacy risks. Industrial and retail asset verification fails when the cloud is unavailable. Project SteelVision is our answer: a 100% offline-first Spatial Intelligence pipeline.

The Architecture

Neural Pipeline

A hybrid vision architecture utilizing YOLOv10 for real-time object detection fused with Vision Transformers (ViT) for high-fidelity OCR.

Local Intelligence

An on-device RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system indexing 2M+ technical manual tokens for instant offline diagnostics.

Hardware Sync

Direct sensor fusion via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE 5.4) to synchronize physical asset states with digital twins in real-time.

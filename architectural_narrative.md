## 3. Technical Core & Flagships

### Technical Core Focus Areas
- **Generative AI & LLMOps:** Agentic Orchestration (LangGraph/CrewAI), parameter-efficient fine-tuning (LoRA/QLoRA), and foundation models (Gemini 1.5 Pro, LLaMA 3.2, GPT-4o).
- **Neural Architecture:** Model Quantization (GGUF/AWQ), CoreML, TFLite, and Metal Performance Shaders (MPS) for high-efficiency edge execution.
- **Computer Vision:** YOLOv10, Vision Transformers (ViT), and Segment Anything (SAM) for spatial verification.
- **Vector Engineering:** Semantic search, Hybrid RAG, and high-performance querying using Vector DBs (ChromaDB, Pinecone, Milvus).

### AI Flagships

**SteelVision: Physical AI & Edge Vision Pipeline**
Architected a production-grade, offline-first Spatial Intelligence system combining OCR with YOLOv10 object detection to verify physical assets (P&IDs) in resource-constrained industrial environments.

**Submission AI: Enterprise Knowledge Retrieval & Multilingual NMT**
Built a global-scale AI platform supporting real-time NMT and semantic indexing. This includes a cross-platform RAG engine using Semantic Chunking and Verse-aware Embeddings to allow sub-200ms querying of massive theological and technical corpora.

**SPEAR: Autonomous Agentic Workflows & Hazard Diagnostics**
Engineered using low-latency BLE-to-ML sensor fusion pipelines for real-time diagnostics, utilizing edge processing (e.g., Fuelpak FP4 strategies) to predict hardware failure modes and anomalies, wrapping protocols in an ambient intelligence layer.
